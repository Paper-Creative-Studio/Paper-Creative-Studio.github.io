Chemistry Geek

Chemistry Geek is an educational game about chemistry. In game you can study, take exams, conduct experiments and modify your character. Chemistry Geek was originally created for GEEK competition orginised by PTI. We submited our project which resulted in getting into the finals.

Game purpose

  • Teaching Chemistry
  • Testing users knowledge
