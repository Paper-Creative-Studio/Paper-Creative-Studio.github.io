Packer

Packer is an app that helps you pack. Everytime you are going somewhere you can use Packer to generate list of items that you need.

Functionalities

  • Generating list of items to pack on your trip based on your preferences
  • Editing list of items
  • Marking packed things
  • Reminding you to pack your items
