Packer

Packer is a web application that help users pack all necessary things for their trip. Packer is built on Laravel framework and availble as Progressive Web Application (PWA). Packer has a form where user answers 10 questions and then uses an algorythm to generate checklist of items that user might need on their trip. You can add and delete positions on that list. After the trip you can submit a survey that modify your item count factor. In that way Packer always modifies itself to personal preferences of exact user.

App purpose

  • Help users pack everything they need
  • Help users with remembering every thing they packed
