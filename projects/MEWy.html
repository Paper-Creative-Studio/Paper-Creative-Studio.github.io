MEWy

MEWy is an international competition organised by Politechnika Morska w Szczecinie that consists in proposing an innovative solutions for offshore wind farms. Our team eliminated the problem of wind farms stability. We suggested connecting offshore wind farms with pumped storage power plant. When there will be an excess of electricity, we would pump the water up, and when there was a shortage, we would let it back down. Pumping water uses 30% of overall energy what is a insignificant cost compared to electicity safety and stability.
