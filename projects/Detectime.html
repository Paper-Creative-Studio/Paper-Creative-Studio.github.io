Detectime

Detectime is a 2D pixel art detective game. It tells a story of british detective named Charles travelling in time to examine famous murders in the past. Detectime was originally made for second edition of nationwide educational game making competition "GEEK" and made it to the finals

Game purpose

  • Teaching about important historical events
  • Development of perceptiveness
  • Development of logical thinking
  • Entertainment
