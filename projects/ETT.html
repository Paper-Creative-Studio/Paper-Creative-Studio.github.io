Exit The Temple

Exit The Temple is a game where player is trapped in the tower and has to escape. The only problem with that is there are more copies of him that moves exactly the same no matter what. Player has to escape the location by touching the ladder and without killing any of players copies.

Game purpose

  • Entertainment
  • Development of logical thinking
