Insight

Insight is a 3D game with fantastic story and a lot of puzzles. It tells a story about blue-haired girl living in post-apocaliptyc city without plants. Someday she found an retro arcade machine and while she was trying to plug it in she fainted and woke up inside machine. Her goal is to break out from machine but everything is trying to keep her inside...

Game purpose

  • Entertainment & chillout
  • Development of logical thinking
